2048 Cube

This is a work in progress and will have many more features optimizations to come (click here to see the list of TODOs). That said, feel free to play the game; feedback is super welcome.

  • WASD: Flips the cube around
  • Arrows: Move the cubes to either left, right, up, or down
  • <: Moves the cubes back (towards the back of the cube)
  • ?: Moves the cubes forward (towards the front of the cube)
